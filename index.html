/// FAQ

Questions you should be asking.

How is this different from hiring an internal ops or AI hire?+

Internal hires are great once you know exactly what to build. Most companies at this stage don't. This gets you the working system first — deployed, stabilised, and understood — then we help you define the right internal person to run it. You're not choosing between this and a hire. You're getting the thing the hire would have taken 6 months to figure out, in 4 weeks.

What happens when something breaks?+

Every Ops Audit and Deployment Sprint includes a 30-day stabilisation window. If something breaks under normal use, we fix it at no extra cost. After that, the Retained Ops plan covers ongoing monitoring and updates. Per-incident support is available at published rates if you opt out.

Do you replace my team?+

No. The goal is to remove the work your team should never have been doing — so the people you hired to think can actually think. The best projects didn't eliminate roles. They eliminated the part of the role that made good people want to leave.

What if we don't know what to automate?+

That's the normal starting point. Most clients come in with "this feels messy" or "we're drowning but can't point to why." The Ops Audit turns that into a specific list: workflows ranked by hours saved, implementation risk, and ROI. You don't need a plan. Arriving with a problem is enough.

Can you work with our existing tools and security constraints?+

Yes. We start from your current stack. No ripping and replacing. No forcing a preferred tool on you. If something can't be done safely with your setup, we'll tell you directly. The goal is systems you own and can run without us.

Who pays for the LLMs and automation infrastructure?+

Everything runs on your stack. Your CRM, your LLM accounts, your infrastructure. We provision and configure it all, but the underlying vendor costs are billed directly to you. Our fees cover scoping, build, deployment, and managed ops. No hidden markup on tooling.
